Tires and Wheels

Cobra Tire - CTR

An aromatic soluble glaze for tires. Permanently protects and recovers the original color, protects the tire from aging and cracking, and prolongs the life of the tire.

Recommended concentration – 1:3

Cobra Tire S - CTRS

A new generation of artistic glaze for tires. Allows operation in higher dilution than the standard glaze, with a "wet tire" effect, and partial water-repellent effect.

Recommended concentration – 1:5 – 1:10

Wheel Cleaner - CWL

A powerful acid detergent for washing aluminum and chrome wheels. Effectively removes dirt and traces of oxidation for a fresh and clean look.

Recommended concentration – 1:5 – 1:10

Silicone

A highly resistant wash and waxing agent that cleans and protects automotive parts. Safe for glass, plastic, and rubber, with a 100% biodegradable formula.

Recommended concentration – ready for use
